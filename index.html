Welcome to WISECONDOR

WISECONDOR is a set of python scripts that attempts to classify fetal aberrations in maternal blood samples. It was developed during my masters thesis at the TU Delft Bioinformatics department in collaboration with VU Medical Center Amsterdam, department of clinical genetics, and is currently used by several medical centers as a tool for non-invasive prenatal diagnostics.

The algorithm used was published in Oxford Journals, Nucleic Acids Research, Online Methods as an Open Access article.
